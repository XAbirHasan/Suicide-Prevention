This figure represents the wordcloud of user status who commits suicide. From the figure we can see that most of the suicidal acts take place due to uncertainty. When people feel insecure and lonely, they feel disappointed. Some of them cannot hold their emotion and the end results is uncertain suicide. We collect some data which was written by the victims before their suicide.
From those data using wordcloud we find out `আমার', `আমি', ` আমাকে', `আমাদের' are the mostly used words which imply possessiveness, uncertainty and insecurity. Some other words are `কিন্তু', `সাথে', `না', `করতে', `মা', `কেউ', `হয়তো' etc.
All these words help to explain their emotional arrogance and thought before being committing suicide.
This figure represents the wordcloud of Bangla News articles. We can see some highlighted words which are used most frequently in the Bangla News articles. We collect those articles to gather information about suicide committers'.
Most newspapers use the following words to publish a suicidal news, `আত্মহত্যা করেছেন', `উপজেলা', `লাশ উদধার', `গলায় ফাঁস', `থানার ওসি', `ময়নাতদন্ত' etc.
All these words help us to find more suicidal data and understand the reason of suicide. Beside that, these data also help us to collect some other information. (i.e. temperature and weather of that day, gratitude and longitude of the area, suicide time etc.)
This figure represents the wordcloud of English News articles. As like Bangla articles we also collect english news articles. We can see some highlighted words which are used most frequently in the English News articles.
We collect those articles to gather information about suicide committers'. From those data using wordcloud we find out most commonly use words to publish a suicidal news are, `committed suicide', `police station', `officer charge', `said', `house', `wife', `upazilla', `village', `mother', `later', `family', `night' etc.
All these words help us to find more suicidal data, understand the reason of suicide, suicide time etc.
